Our Methodology

From first call
to final bloom.

Our six-step process removes the guesswork from landscaping. We ensure every project is thoughtfully designed, transparently priced, cleanly executed, and lovingly maintained.

Why our process works

By separating design from installation, we give you full control. You see exactly what you’re getting via 3D renders before a single shovel hits the dirt.

Start your journey
01

Discovery & Vision

We begin with a thorough 45-minute consultation to understand your aesthetic preferences, lifestyle needs, budget framework, and long-term goals for the space.

02

Site Survey & Science

Our engineers visit your site to analyze the microclimate. We test soil pH and drainage, map sun trajectories, check wind exposure, and locate utility lines to inform our botanical choices.

03

Master Concept Design

You receive a comprehensive design package including a high-resolution mood board, a specific plant species palette, 2D layout blueprints, and immersive 3D visualizations of the finished space.

04

Procurement

Upon approval, we hand-select premium specimens from our network of ethical nurseries. We source high-grade soil mixes, organic fertilizers, and custom fabricated planters specific to your design.

05

Clean Installation

Our landscaping crews execute the build with military precision. We handle everything from hardscaping drops to irrigation plumbing, ensuring your property is left spotless after planting.

06

Aftercare & Growth

A landscape is alive; it requires stewardship. We provide a 30-day establishment guarantee and offer ongoing, seasonal maintenance contracts to protect your investment as the garden matures.
