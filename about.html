Origin Story

Born from a simple realization: cities were suffocating.

In 2014, our founder Dr. Rohan Mehta noticed a disturbing trend in rapid urban development. Modern architecture was pushing nature entirely out of the blueprint, relegating "landscaping" to an afterthought of chemical-heavy, unsustainable lawns.

Terra's World started as a small consultancy aimed at re-introducing native, indigenous plant species back into urban balconies and terraces. We didn't want to just sell potted plants; we wanted to establish micro-ecosystems that attracted pollinators and managed water efficiently.

Today, our studio comprises structural engineers, soil scientists, botanists, and master designers. We tackle everything from hyper-dense commercial living walls that purify office air, to sprawling 10-acre farmhouse permaculture master plans.

Philosophy

The principles that root us.

Ecological First

Beautiful design cannot come at the cost of the environment. We strictly source non-invasive species, optimize for extremely low water consumption through xeriscaping, and employ 100% organic soil remediation.

Native Vitality

We prioritize indigenous flora that is genetically predisposed to thrive in local soil and climate conditions. This drastically reduces the need for synthetic fertilizers and intensive maintenance interventions.

Human Biophilia

We believe that human beings have an innate, biological need to connect with nature. Our spaces are designed not just to be looked at, but to be inhabited, felt, smelled, and touched to lower cortisol levels.
