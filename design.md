Design a modern, nature-forward website with a clean and minimal aesthetic. The visual language should feel botanical, premium, and editorial. Use generous whitespace, soft off-white backgrounds, and pair them with rich dark green imagery. Avoid heavy drop shadows or gradients. All surfaces are flat. The design should feel calm, trustworthy, and slightly upscale — targeting urban professionals and design-conscious customers.

Primary background: #F5F3EF (warm off-white cream). Card backgrounds: white #FFFFFF. Text primary: #1A1A18 (near-black). Feature card accents — use four distinct soft pastels:

Sticky top navbar. Background: warm off-white (#F5F3EF), no border or line separator. Logo sits on the far left — monogram-style icon with a small leaf/hat motif, rendered in dark green. Nav links on the far right are displayed as pill-shaped chips with a thin 0.5px gray border, transparent fill, and subtle hover state. No hamburger unless mobile. Font: 14px, weight 400–450, sentence case. Spacing between nav items: 8px. The navbar should feel airy, not heavy.

Full-width hero with a dark botanical photograph as background (deep green monstera/tropical leaves, high contrast, slightly dark-toned). Overlay: subtle dark gradient from left to ensure text legibility — no solid color block. Rounded corners on the hero container (border-radius ~16px). Content is left-aligned, positioned in the upper-left quadrant. Heading: ~36–40px, white, font-weight 600, multi-line (2–3 lines). Subheading: ~14px, white at 70% opacity, max-width ~420px, line-height 1.6. CTA button: pill-shaped, white background, dark text, with a diagonal arrow icon on the right side using a circular dark accent bubble. No other elements over the image. Aspect ratio: ~3:1 landscape.

Four equal-width rounded cards in a single horizontal row below the hero. Each card is a photo card with a real-life interior/plant photograph filling the entire card. Cards have border-radius ~12px, equal height (~200px), and small equal gaps (8–10px) between them. At the bottom-left corner of each card, overlay a white pill-shaped label badge (e.g. "For office", "For home", "For restaurant", "For any space") with 12–13px text, dark color, subtle background, padded ~8px 14px. No hover animations by default. The photos should feel editorial — natural light, interior spaces with plants.

Two-column layout. Left column: Large editorial heading, ~44–52px, dark near-black, weight 600, 3 lines tall (e.g. "Why our service is the best solution?"). No subtext in this column — let the heading breathe. Right column: A 2×2 grid of pastel feature cards. Each card has a soft colored background (sage green, warm khaki, blush pink, sky blue — one per card), rounded corners (border-radius ~14px), and contains: a short bold heading (~16px, weight 500) and 2–3 lines of body text (~13–14px, same color family but darker). No icons, no borders, no shadows. Padding inside cards: ~20px. Card gap: ~12px.

Use a clean geometric sans-serif throughout (e.g. Inter, DM Sans, or similar). Headings: weight 600–700 for h1 hero, 600 for section headings, 500 for card headings. Body text: weight 400, color at 60–70% opacity of primary. No serif fonts. Font sizes: hero heading ~36–40px, section heading ~44–52px, card heading ~16px, body ~13–14px, nav items ~14px, badge labels ~12px. Line-height: 1.2–1.3 for headings, 1.6–1.7 for body. Letter-spacing: normal for body, slightly tight (-0.01em) for large headings.

Page max-width: ~1200px, centered. Horizontal page padding: 40px on desktop. Section vertical spacing: 48–64px between major sections. The overall layout should feel roomy and uncluttered. No full-bleed sections other than the hero. All subsequent sections sit on the warm cream background. Content sections use an internal content column (not full-width edge-to-edge). Keep consistent 12–16px gaps between cards and grid items.
